About Nulyf Foundation

Nulyf Foundation is a non-profit organization dedicated to improving lives and building stronger communities. Our mission is to provide education, healthcare, and economic opportunities to those in need.

Since our inception in 2010, we have impacted over 100,000 lives across 50 communities. Our dedicated team of volunteers and partners work tirelessly to create sustainable change and empower individuals to reach their full potential.

Founder & CEO - Dr. Prerit Mathur

Dr. Prerit Mathur, a distinguished healthcare professional with over 12 years of experience, leads Nulyf Foundation with a vision to transform communities through sustainable development and compassionate service. As a Senior Consultant in Pediatric Surgery and Pediatric Urology at Sir Ganga Ram Hospital, he brings his medical expertise and humanitarian values to guide our mission.

Under his leadership, Nulyf Foundation has expanded its reach across multiple programs including healthcare initiatives, education support, and community welfare projects. His dedication to serving others and commitment to excellence continues to inspire our foundation's work in creating lasting positive change.

Our Programs

Education for All

Providing quality education and learning resources to underprivileged children in rural India, empowering them to build a brighter future.

Community Health

Improving access to healthcare services and promoting wellness in rural areas, ensuring better health outcomes for underserved communities.

Economic Empowerment

Supporting small businesses and providing vocational training for sustainable livelihoods, helping families break the cycle of poverty.

Cow Seva

Protecting and caring for cows, promoting sustainable dairy practices, and supporting local farmers to preserve India's cultural heritage.

Community Kitchen

Providing nutritious meals to those in need and fostering a sense of community through shared dining experiences, combating hunger and malnutrition.
